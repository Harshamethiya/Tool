About FileToolsPro

Empowering users with free, secure, and easy-to-use online tools for all their file processing needs since 2020.

Our Story

FileToolsPro was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, free online tools that make working with images and PDFs easier for everyone.

What started as a small collection of basic tools has grown into a comprehensive platform serving millions of users worldwide. Our team of developers and designers is committed to continuously improving and expanding our toolset.

We believe that powerful file processing tools should be accessible to everyone, regardless of technical skill or budget. That's why all our core tools remain completely free to use.

Our Technology

All FileToolsPro tools are built using modern web technologies that process files directly in your browser. This approach provides several key benefits:

Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security. Processing happens instantly without waiting for uploads to servers. And there's no software to install - everything works right in your web browser.

We use advanced algorithms to ensure the highest quality results while maintaining fast processing speeds. Our tools are regularly updated to support the latest file formats and standards.
