Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about image conversion

What image formats can I convert to?

Our image converter supports conversion to and from JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, and TIFF formats. You can convert between any of these formats with just a few clicks.

Is there a file size limit for conversion?

You can convert images up to 20MB in size. For best results with large files, we recommend using our desktop application available for download.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

How can I maintain image quality during conversion?

Use the quality slider to adjust the output quality. Higher values preserve more quality but result in larger file sizes. For lossless formats like PNG, quality settings don't affect the image data.

Is this tool completely free to use?

Yes, our image converter is 100% free with no hidden charges or watermarks. We may show ads to support the service, but the functionality is completely free.
