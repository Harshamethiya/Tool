Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about image resizing

Will resizing affect my image quality?

Resizing can affect quality, especially when making images larger. Our tool uses high-quality algorithms to minimize quality loss. For best results, avoid enlarging images beyond their original dimensions.

What's the maximum image size I can resize?

You can resize images up to 20MB in size. For best results with very large files, we recommend resizing in stages or using our desktop application.

What does "Maintain aspect ratio" mean?

When this option is checked, changing one dimension will automatically adjust the other to keep the original proportions of your image. Uncheck it if you want to stretch or squeeze the image to exact dimensions.

Which format should I choose for my resized image?

JPG is best for photographs, PNG for images with transparency, and WebP for modern web use with good compression. Quality settings affect JPG and WebP files more than PNG.

Is this tool completely free to use?

Yes, our image resizer is 100% free with no hidden charges or watermarks. We may show ads to support the service, but the functionality is completely free.
