How to Convert Images to ZIP

1

Select Images

Click the "Select Images" button or drag and drop your image files into the upload area. You can select multiple images at once.

2

Review Your Files

Check the list of selected images to make sure you've included all the files you want in your ZIP archive.

3

Create ZIP Archive

Click the "Create ZIP" button to compress all your selected images into a single ZIP file.

4

Download Your ZIP

Once processing is complete, your browser will automatically download the ZIP file containing all your images.
