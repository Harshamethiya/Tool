Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PDF compression

Will compression affect my PDF quality?

Compression can affect quality, especially at higher levels. Our tool uses smart algorithms to minimize quality loss while maximizing file size reduction. For important documents, we recommend testing different compression levels.

What's the maximum PDF size I can compress?

You can compress PDFs up to 50MB in size. For best results with very large files, we recommend compressing in stages or using our desktop application.

How much can I reduce my PDF file size?

Reduction depends on the original file. Image-heavy PDFs can often be reduced by 50-80%, while text-based PDFs typically see 20-50% reduction. Extreme compression can achieve even greater savings but may affect quality.

Is my PDF content secure during compression?

Yes, all processing happens in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security. We don't store or access your documents after processing.

Is this tool completely free to use?

Yes, our PDF compressor is 100% free with no hidden charges or watermarks. We may show ads to support the service, but the functionality is completely free.
