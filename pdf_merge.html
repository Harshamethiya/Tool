Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PDF merging

How many PDFs can I merge at once?

You can merge up to 20 PDF files at once, with a total size limit of 50MB. For larger merging tasks, we recommend using our desktop application.

Will the merged PDF maintain original quality?

Yes, the merged PDF will maintain the original quality of all input files. We don't compress or modify the content during the merging process.

Can I rearrange pages after merging?

Yes, you can use our PDF Splitter tool to extract pages and then merge them again in your preferred order if needed.

Are my PDFs secure during merging?

Absolutely. All processing happens in your browser. Your files never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Is this tool completely free to use?

Yes, our PDF merger is 100% free with no hidden charges or watermarks. We may show ads to support the service, but the functionality is completely free.
