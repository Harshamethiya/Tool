About PDF Splitting

How to split a PDF document

Our PDF splitter tool allows you to divide your PDF document in several ways:

  • By page ranges: Specify exactly which pages or ranges of pages you want to extract
  • Extract specific pages: Select individual pages from anywhere in the document
  • By file size: Automatically split the PDF into parts of a specified maximum size

Why split a PDF?

Splitting PDFs can be useful for many reasons:

  • Extract only the pages you need from a large document
  • Divide a scanned book into chapters
  • Separate contracts or agreements into individual documents
  • Reduce file size for email attachments
  • Organize documents by topic or section

Is it secure?

Absolutely! Your files are processed entirely in your browser. We don't upload your documents to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.
