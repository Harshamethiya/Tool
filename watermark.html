How to Add Watermarks to Your Photos

1

Upload Your Image

Click the "Select Image" button to choose the photo you want to watermark from your device.

2

Choose Watermark Type

Select between text or image watermark using the tabs. For text watermarks, enter your text and customize its appearance. For image watermarks, upload your logo or watermark image.

3

Customize Your Watermark

Adjust the position, size, opacity, rotation, and other settings to create the perfect watermark for your needs.

4

Apply and Download

Click "Apply Watermark" to see the result, then "Download Image" to save your watermarked photo to your device.
